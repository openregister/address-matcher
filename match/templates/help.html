Help

Instructions

Your goal is to find the definitive address of job centres across the UK.

When you start the game, you’ll see the name and address of a job centre on the left-hand side. On the right, you’ll see 9 different addresses from AddressBase.

Click on one of the 9 addresses you think matches the address of the job centre.

If none match, click ‘No match’ and if you’re unsure, click ‘Not sure’ to move onto the next job centre.

You only need to match the addresses - the name of the job centre is not important. The more matches you make and the more accurate they are, the more you score.

Background

The address on the left hand-side is from a list of UK job centres. The 9 addresses on the right are drawn from AddressBase data containing a Unique Property Reference Number (UPRN). We want to find the UPRN of each job centre, by matching the job centre’s text address on the left to the correct UPRN-associated address on the right.

Matching addresses is hard. Automated matching only takes us so far, and typically misses the most complex addresses. This tool takes the crowd-sourcing approach: participants are asked to match addresses manually from a list of possible candidates.

Your score will reflect the number of matches you’ve made, as well as how well your guesses match other people’s. Enough matches will allow us to establish with a good degree of certainty the correct answer.
